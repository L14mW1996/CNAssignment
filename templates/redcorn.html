Baby Red Corn Snake

Corn Snakes are perfect first snake pets for any age. Beautifully patterned, this red corn snake will grow to a maximum of 6ft. They eat small rodents and are a low maintainence pet, who also love to be handled by humans. It is recommended to buy a Vivarium to house them in, using substrate on the bottom giving them somewhere to cool down and hide, along with some logs to climb around on and a sheltered area to sleep in. heat lamp is necessary as they need a consistent temperature of around 28 to 32 degrees to survive.
